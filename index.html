About this map

Data:
U.S. bankruptcy indicators by court district, including chapter totals, business and nonbusiness counts, shares, per-capita values, and change measures.

DataSource:
U.S. bankruptcy, court district, and population:
US Supreme Court
https://www.uscourts.gov/data-news/reports/statistical-reports/bankruptcy-filings-statistics
Platform:
This map is built with OpenLayers and hosted as a lightweight static website.

Design note:
The site is intentionally simple so it can remain portable, fast to load, and workable on a limited free hosting setup.
